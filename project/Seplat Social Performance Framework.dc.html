The Brief

Two audiences, one social story

Seplat is listed on both the Nigerian Exchange and the London Stock Exchange. Its social performance has to speak to Nigerian regulators and international investors — from a single, credible set of data.

The Local Lens
License to operate

PIA 2021 & Host Community Trusts, NCDMB local content, NUPRC and NEITI. What Nigeria requires to keep the social license in the Niger Delta.

The Global Lens
Access to capital

GRI, IFRS / ISSB, IPIECA and the UN SDGs. The frameworks London investors and lenders use to price ESG risk and allocate capital.

The Gap
Activity, not impact

Reporting today counts what was spent and how many were trained. It rarely shows the lives changed — or the risk reduced.
